How it's made: Good Morning Tech

Designed with

Figma, Adobe Photoshop and Dribbble

Built with

Python for the backend. HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript for the frontend

Tech Stack

For the backend we used Flask together with MongoDB for the Database. For the frontend we used Jinja Templates and Tailwind. For the website hosting we used Vercel and for the emails and Images we used Cybrancee.
For a more detailed technical breakdown, check out Levani's article about it. (not yet written)

Disclaimer news content

We would like to take a moment to clarify that not all of the news content on our website is our original work. As a news aggregator, we strive to provide our readers with the latest and most accurate news from various reliable sources. While we do publish some original content, a significant portion of the news articles you will find on our website are sourced from other news outlets. We carefully select and curate the news stories that we believe are most relevant and informative to our readers, and we always provide proper attribution to the original sources. We encourage our readers to visit the original sources of the news articles we publish to gain a more comprehensive understanding of the topics covered.
